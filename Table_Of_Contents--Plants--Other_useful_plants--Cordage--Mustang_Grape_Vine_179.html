Mustang Grape Vine


Rip off the outer bark,then score a ring around the vine at the end of one of the straightest,limb free sections,then score another ring around it at whatever distance from the first cut you want (depending on how long you can get it) and the inner bark should easily peel off in long strips,soak em" first then braid/twist em up.its strong and not to brittle when dried.
