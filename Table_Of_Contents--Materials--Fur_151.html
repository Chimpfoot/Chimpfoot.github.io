Fur


Fur can be found on recent carcasses or ideally on animals you have harvested yourself.

The fur-bearing animals on the property are: badgers, foxes, opossums, raccoons, skunks, coyotes and bobcats. Other animals are either too small or are more ideal for leather.

Fur is ideal for keeping warm. To use furs you need to tan only the furless side of the skin.
